About me

I am a passionate Software Engineering undergraduate at the University of Westminster, studying at the Informatics Institute of Technology, Sri Lanka. With experience as a Software Engineer Intern and Service Desk Analyst at HCL Technologies, I have gained hands-on expertise in problem-solving, system management, and software development. These experiences have strengthened my technical foundation while enhancing my ability to create user-friendly digital solutions.

My true passion lies in UI/UX design and mobile application development. Over time, I have explored Flutter as my primary framework for building cross-platform mobile applications, allowing me to create smooth, responsive, and visually appealing apps. The ability to transform ideas into functional and engaging mobile experiences excites me, and I am continuously refining my skills to craft human-centered digital products. As I grow in my journey, I strive to merge design and technology to build seamless, impactful digital solutions.

About (The other side of) me

As I continue this journey, I strive to use my voice to inspire, educate, and contribute meaningful discussions to the community.
